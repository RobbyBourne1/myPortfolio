About Me

Thank you for visiting my page and I am excited to show off the work that I have done since joining the Iron Yard. But first, a little more about myself. I am a Florida native born and raised in Port St Lucie, Florida. Then migrated to Tampa to attend the University of South Florida (Go Bulls!!!). I studied Business Management there and after graduating entered the world of recruitment working with people all over the United States helping them find their next challenge. I soon realized that I was looking for the next challenge myself so I ended up enrolling at the Iron Yard. Now I didn’t just do that on a whim my Dad is an IT director for a non-profit organization in Port St Lucie. So, In other words, I have been around computers and Technology my whole life it just took me a bit to decide that I wanted to make a career out of it.

When I’m not trying to learn code you can either find me playing, watching sports, playing sports or watching sports video games in my spare time. Having grown up playing baseball and football I feel that it has taught me a lot in the ways or competition, problem-solving, and teamwork that has helped me thus far in my career. More information about my career path and ways to contact me are listed here. I look forward to connecting and feel free to ask any questions that you may have about my portfolio and projects listed here on my page.
